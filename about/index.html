About Me

As a piano instructor with over 15 years of experience playing piano, I’ve developed an understanding of not only tone, but also how a piano responds to its player. This musical background, combined with training in both aural tuning and hands-on technical work, directly informs my approach to tuning and regulation, ensuring each instrument is adjusted with both technical precision and musical sensitivity.

My goal is to provide reliable, detail-focused piano service that supports both the instrument and the player.

My Approach

I focus on careful evaluation of each instrument’s condition, with close attention to pitch stability, tone, and touch. From there, I walk you through what I find and provide straightforward options so you can make informed decisions about how to move forward.

Every piano and player are different, so my approach is never one size fits all. Whether an instrument is used for daily practice, teaching, performance, or family use, the goal is always to improve both musical responsiveness and long-term reliability while respecting the character of the piano itself.
