White Piano Services provides professional piano tuning and repair services for homeowners, music teachers, schools, and studios throughout Chester, Delaware and Montgomery counties.

About Me

Music has always been a guiding force in my life. As a multi-instrumentalist and piano instructor with over 15 years of experience playing piano, I’ve developed a deep understanding of not only how music sounds, but how an instrument responds to its player.

Outside of music, I’ve spent years woodworking, developing a respect for precision, materials, and process. That same mindset carries directly into my work with pianos. It’s this combination of musical sensitivity and technical precision that allows me to bring out the best in every instrument I work on.

My Approach

I focus on careful evaluation of each instrument’s condition, with close attention to pitch stability, tone, touch, and mechanical function.

I’ll walk you through its condition and provide clear, practical options so you can make informed decisions about how to move forward.
