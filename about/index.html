About Me

As a piano instructor with over 15 years of experience playing piano, I’ve developed a deep awareness of not only sound, but also how an instrument responds to its player. This musical background directly informs my approach to tuning and regulation, ensuring each instrument is adjusted with both technical precision and musical sensitivity.

My goal is to provide reliable, detail-focused piano service that supports both the instrument and the player.

My Approach

I focus on careful evaluation of each instrument’s condition, with close attention to pitch stability, tone, touch, and mechanical function. From there, I walk you through what I find in clear, practical terms and provide straightforward options so you can make informed decisions about how to move forward.
