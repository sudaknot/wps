SERVICES

Tuning

A fundamental part of maintaining a piano.

Precise tuning bringing balance to interval relationships and stability to unisons, resulting in a more harmonious overall sound.

Pianos that haven’t been tuned in over a year may require a pitch raise.

Cleaning

Focused on the soundboard, plate, keys, and internal components to remove dust and buildup that can affect performance.

This helps preserve the instrument’s condition while maintaining a cleaner, more responsive playing experience.

Repair

Having an issue with your piano?

Address mechanical and performance problems that can develop over time.

Each repair is handled with careful attention to detail to ensure your instrument performs at its best.
